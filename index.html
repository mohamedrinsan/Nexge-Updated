    Welcome to Nexge

    We have been pioneering the efforts to manufacture and distribute packaging products for a variety of industries that include consumer packaged goods, Retail, Hospitality, Healthcare, Pharma and Construction. In a very short span of 26 years, we have become the largest manufacturer in the region for diversified products that include paper, aluminum and plastic. The operations include 12 manufacturing plants having more than 2 Million Sq.ft. of manufacturing area with state-of-the-art equipment and logistics & warehousing support. Currently we also produce disposable products in eco-friendly and biodegradable categories. We are present in more than 25 locations that include Middle East, UK, North America and most of African countries. We strive to deliver value by offering highest quality at affordable pricing. Our strength is our relationship management that has enabled long customer retention. All our plants are having relevant international quality certifications and are having experienced personnel to maintain Good Manufacturing Practices. All this has enabled our exports to expand to more than 95 countries worldwide.
